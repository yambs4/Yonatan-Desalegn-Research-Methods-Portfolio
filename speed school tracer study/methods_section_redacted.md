## 1. Evaluation Design

A mixed‑methods quasi‑experimental design, integrating quantitative and qualitative evidence for triangulation and causal inference.

---

## 2. Quantitative Methods

### 2.1 Study Groups
- **Treatment group:** individuals exposed to the intervention.  
- **Comparison group:** similar individuals with no exposure.

### 2.2 Sampling
A three‑stage clustered sampling process:
1. Select regions/districts where the intervention was implemented.  
2. Randomly select schools/communities.  
3. Randomly sample individual respondents within clusters.

### 2.3 Outcome Variables
- Transition, retention, dropout  
- Performance scores  
- Household welfare indicators  
- Aspirations  

### 2.4 Analytical Strategy
- Descriptive analysis  
- Means comparisons  
- PSM (nearest‑neighbor & kernel)  
- Robustness checks using different methods of measuring student performance

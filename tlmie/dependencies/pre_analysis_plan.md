# 1. Pre‑Analysis Plan (NDA‑Compliant)

This pre‑analysis plan (PAP) outlines the evaluation design, hypotheses, indicators, and analytic strategy without referencing sensitive project details.

---

## 1.1. Evaluation Objectives

1. Assess changes in:

    - Individual participation in community and household decision‑making
    - Household welfare indicators (e.g., food consumption patterns)
    - Organizational capacity for inclusive governance
    - Public authority practices relating to inclusive decision‑making

2. Estimate project‑attributable effects using quasi‑experimental matching.

---

## 1.2. Primary Outcomes (Generalized)


- **Participation Restriction Index**
  
  Composite score built from multiple Likert‑based items.
  
- **Household Food Indicators**
  
  (E.g., number of food groups consumed, dietary diversity, food source patterns.)
  
- **Organizational Capacity Scores**
  
  Based on multi‑dimension Likert responses regarding planning, coordination, outreach, etc.
  
- **Inclusiveness Measures for Local Authorities**
  
  Self‑assessment scores across structured domains.

## 1.4. Estimation Strategy

- Descriptive statistics with confidence intervals
- Bivariate comparisons (t‑tests, chi‑square tests)
- Causal estimation:

    - Propensity Score Matching (logit model)
    - Nearest‑neighbor or kernel matching
    - ATT (Average Treatment Effect on the Treated)

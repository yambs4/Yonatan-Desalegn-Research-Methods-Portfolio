# 2. Sampling Strategy (Fully Redacted

---

## 2.1. Sampling Frame

The sampling frame consisted of households and organizations eligible for program participation across several administrative clusters.

---

## 2.2. Quantitative Sampling

A probability‑based design ensured representativeness.

Final sample target derived using a standard sample size formula for proportions:

$$n = \frac{Z^2 \times p(1 - p)}{\epsilon^2}$$

- A buffer was added for non‑response.
- Observations were split between:
  - Participants
  - Non‑participants (comparison group) with similar demographic characteristics.

---

## 2.3. Qualitative Sampling

Purposive sampling of:

- Community groups
- Civil society representatives
- Local governance actors
- Project staff
